# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the prohibited activities and conduct standards for users of Free For NonProfits (operated by DVLMNT). By using the Service, you agree to comply with this policy.

## 1. Prohibited Activities

You agree not to use the Service in any manner that:

### 1.1 Illegal Activities
- Violates any applicable federal, state, local, or international law or regulation
- Involves fraud, embezzlement, theft, or financial crimes
- Constitutes harassment, stalking, or abuse
- Involves threats or violence
- Infringes intellectual property rights
- Violates privacy or data protection laws
- Involves child exploitation or abuse

### 1.2 System Interference
- Attempts to gain unauthorized access to the Service or systems
- Disrupts the normal operation of the Service
- Overloads servers with excessive requests (DDoS attacks)
- Exploits vulnerabilities or bugs
- Interferes with network connectivity or functionality
- Transmits malware, viruses, or harmful code
- Uses automated tools to scrape or harvest data without authorization

### 1.3 Fraudulent or Deceptive Conduct
- Misrepresents your identity or nonprofit status
- Submits false or misleading information about tools
- Creates fake accounts or impersonates others
- Engages in phishing or social engineering
- Manipulates reviews or ratings artificially
- Promotes fraudulent schemes or scams

### 1.4 Spam and Abuse
- Sends unsolicited commercial emails or communications
- Posts repetitive, irrelevant, or off-topic content
- Exploits the messaging or notification system
- Engages in posting chains or multi-level marketing schemes
- Creates content designed to manipulate search rankings

### 1.5 Harmful Content
- Posts content containing hate speech, discrimination, or racism
- Promotes violence, self-harm, or illegal activities
- Shares sexually explicit or obscene content
- Discloses other users' private information without consent
- Posts defamatory, libelous, or false statements about others
- Harasses, bullies, or threatens other users

### 1.6 Intellectual Property Violations
- Uploads or shares copyrighted content without authorization
- Violates trademark rights of others
- Shares trade secrets or confidential information
- Uses other users' content without attribution or permission

## 2. Product-Specific Rules

### 2.1 Tool Submissions

You agree to:
- Only submit tools that serve legitimate nonprofit purposes
- Provide accurate, complete, and current information about tools
- Not submit duplicate entries for the same tool
- Not submit tools owned by you for self-promotion without disclosure
- Not submit tools that violate our content standards
- Verify that you have the right to submit the tool information

You agree not to:
- Submit tools that are illegal or harmful
- Submit tools with false or misleading descriptions
- Spam the directory with irrelevant or duplicate submissions
- Use tool submissions for paid advertising or promotional purposes
- Include contact information or URLs for affiliate links in descriptions

### 2.2 Reviews and Ratings

You agree to:
- Post honest reviews based on actual experience with the tool
- Focus your reviews on the tool's features, functionality, and value
- Be respectful and constructive in feedback
- Comply with content standards outlined in Section 1.5

You agree not to:
- Post reviews for tools you have not actually used
- Post fake positive reviews (as the tool creator) or fake negative reviews (to damage competitors)
- Include promotional links or solicitations in reviews
- Post reviews of unrelated products or services
- Manipulate ratings through coordinated fake reviews
- Include personal contact information or external links in review text
- Use reviews to promote your own products or services
- Post multiple reviews of the same tool to inflate appearance of popularity

### 2.3 Account Behavior

You agree to:
- Maintain accurate account information
- Use only one account per person or organization
- Respect the accounts and content of other users
- Report violations to DVLMNT via levi@dvlmnt.com

You agree not to:
- Create multiple accounts to circumvent restrictions or manipulate rankings
- Share your account credentials with others
- Use another person's account without authorization
- Attempt to access other users' accounts or data

## 3. Content Standards

All user-generated content, including reviews, tool submissions, and comments, must:

3.1 **Be Honest and Accurate:** Content must not contain false, misleading, or fraudulent information.

3.2 **Be Respectful:** Content must not demean, harass, or attack individuals or groups.

3.3 **Be Relevant:** Content must be relevant to the Service and the tool being discussed.

3.4 **Be Lawful:** Content must not violate any applicable laws or regulations.

3.5 **Respect Intellectual Property:** Content must not infringe on the intellectual property rights of others.

3.6 **Protect Privacy:** Content must not disclose private information about individuals or organizations without consent.

## 4. Enforcement and Consequences

### 4.1 Monitoring
DVLMNT reserves the right to monitor user activity and content for compliance with this AUP. We may use automated tools and manual review to detect violations.

### 4.2 Content Removal
We may remove, suspend, or disable content that violates this AUP, including:
- Inaccurate or misleading tool submissions
- Violative reviews or comments
- Spam or repetitive content
- Content containing prohibited material

Content removal will occur without prior notice when the violation is clear. Where appropriate, we will notify the user and provide an opportunity to remedy the situation.

### 4.3 Account Warnings
For minor violations, we may issue a warning to the user, describing the violation and requesting corrective action within a specified timeframe.

### 4.4 Account Suspension
For serious or repeated violations, we may suspend your account temporarily, restricting your ability to:
- Submit new tools
- Post reviews
- Create new submissions
- Access certain features

Suspension will be proportional to the nature and severity of the violation.

### 4.5 Account Termination
We may permanently terminate your account and access to the Service if you:
- Repeatedly violate this AUP despite warnings
- Commit serious violations (fraud, illegal activity, severe harassment)
- Attempt to circumvent account suspension restrictions
- Pose a threat to the security or integrity of the Service

### 4.6 Legal Action
For serious violations, including illegal activity, we may pursue legal action and cooperate with law enforcement.

## 5. Reporting Violations

5.1 If you observe a violation of this AUP, please report it to DVLMNT at levi@dvlmnt.com with:
- Description of the violation
- URL or reference to the violating content
- Your contact information
- Any supporting documentation

5.2 We will investigate reported violations promptly and take appropriate action.

5.3 False or bad-faith reports may result in warnings or account suspension.

## 6. Appeals

6.1 If your account has been suspended or terminated, you may appeal the decision by contacting levi@dvlmnt.com within 30 days.

6.2 Your appeal should include:
- Explanation of why you believe the action was inappropriate
- Evidence supporting your position
- Commitment to future compliance

6.3 We will review appeals within 14 days and notify you of the outcome.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

DVLMNT reserves the right to modify this AUP at any time. Changes will be effective upon posting to the website. Your continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.

## 8. Miscellaneous

8.1 **Compliance with Laws:** Regardless of this AUP, you agree to comply with all applicable laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.

8.2 **Severability:** If any provision of this AUP is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions will continue in effect.

8.3 **No Waiver:** Failure to enforce this AUP in specific instances does not constitute a waiver of DVLMNT's rights to enforce it in other instances.
